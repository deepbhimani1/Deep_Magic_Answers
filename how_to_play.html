Instructions

Step 1

  • Type your question in the question box

  • Example: "What is my name?" or "What color is my best friend's shirt?"

Step 2

  • Type in the request prompt to apply to Deep Magic

  • Prompt: "Deep can you please answer the following question"

Step 3

  • Click on submit and wait for the response from Deep Magic!

  • Note: Deep Magic is not always in the mood to answer your questions, they answers when they want to.
